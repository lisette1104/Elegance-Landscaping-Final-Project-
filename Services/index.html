Services

Elegance Landscaping provides free estimates to clients by visiting their sites and listening to their wants and needs. The company is specialized in design, installation, renewal and maintenance of gardens, with extensive experience in caring for green residential and commercial areas. Some of the special services that Elegance Landscaping provides include pond installations, retaining walls, seeding, sodding, outdoor lighting, drainage control, design installation, mulching, tree and stump removal, and renovations. Elegance Landscaping offers all types of lawn maintenance, along with basic lawn care and pruning, at an affordable price by a convenient, local company.
