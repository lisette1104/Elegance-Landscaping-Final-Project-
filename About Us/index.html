About Us

Elegance Landscaping was founded in Fort Lauderdale, Florida by a young entrepreneur who had experience in other areas of constructing a business yet, very little experience in landscaping. The company was established with the vision of fulfilling people’s need of lawn care maintenance in an area where it is of high demand. It began building clientele through caring for the homes of friends and family, which later led to the vast growth of the company. Elegance Landscaping provides consumers with the opportunity to have a cared for and appealing environment. “
